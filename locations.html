Sexton Square

The Sexton Square, usually referred to as the palms by students. It's right at the center of the NYU Abu Dhabi campus and the place to be when the weather is nice.

Chilling under the shade of the palm trees, be it alone with your favorite book or accompanied by friends and campus cats, is like an NYU Abu Dhabi ritual.

Featured in the second scene, when Nada runs into Alexa as she's walking to C2.

Campus Center

As the name suggests, Campus Center is the focal point of NYU Abu Dhabi. Students usually refer to this one as C2!

The building hosts many offices, meeting rooms, lounges, study spaces, our mailroom, as well as so much more. Most importantly (at least in the context of our film), on the first floor of the Campus Center, you'll find the Convenience Store.

This is where Nada awkwardly avoided Maitha on her way up the stairs to the Convenience Store.

Convenience Store

Commonly referred to as CS, the Convenience Store is, well, the most convenient spot for you to buy all your essentials on campus.

The Convenience Store accepts Campus Dirhams, Falcon Dirhams, and real money, cash and credit. From snacks to notebooks to chargers, the convenience store is open everyday for students (and visitors) to purchase whatever they may need.

This is where Nada was trying to get throughout our film. It is also where she awkwardly ran into Maitha, a second time.
